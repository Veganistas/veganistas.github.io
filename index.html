VEGAN PRE-THANKSGIVING POTLUCK & RECIPE SWAP

Saturday, November 9th, 6:30 PM

At the First Unitarian Universalist Church of Austin - Howson Hall, 4700 Grover Ave. Austin, TX 78756

  • Bring a VEGAN* dish that serves eight, something festive, or a veganized version of a holiday classic, or your best unique specialty
  • Share YOUR vegan holiday recipe and find new ideas here
  • ALL DISHES NEED TO BE VEGAN* It is helpful to bring a list of ingredients or copies of your recipe! As a courtesy, be prepared to give information about allergens.
  • Email to sign-up or for more information info@veganistas.org
  • Members of AVVA and Veg Austin Meetup register at Meetup.com
  • Co-sponsored by the Veganistas of First UU Austin & The Austin Vegan and Vegetarian Association

*ALL DISHES NEED TO BE VEGAN, meaning no animal products at all. (No meat, fish, eggs, or dairy products including no milk, cheese, yogurt or butter, no animal broth, gelatin, or honey. Please check the ingredients of everything used in your dish.) Be prepared to give information about allergens.

About

The Veganistas are a diverse group of individuals from the First Unitarian Universalist Church of Austin. We are united in our mission to support one another and make veganism more accessible. Through social gatherings, planned events, and knowledge sharing, we foster a welcoming environment for the church and community to explore and embrace veganism.

Mission

Our mission is to enthusiastically build interest, raise awareness, and enhance accessibility to veganism within the church and community.
